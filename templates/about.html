Getting a Printer

The Distributed Press publishes to any Google Cloud Print enabled device, which includes most wifi-enabled printers. Many are available online for around $60.

You can also publish to any traditional printer by connecting it to a computer running the Google Cloud Print bridge.

Subscribing

Once your cloud printer is connected to Google Cloud Print, simply login to the Distributed Press and add it from your dashboard. You'll receive future issues of the Distributed Press straight to your printer!

If you wish to unsubscribe, you can remove your printer from your dashboard, or disconnect the Distributed Press from your Google Account Settings under "Connected applications and sites"
